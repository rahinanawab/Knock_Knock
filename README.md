# Knock_Knock

# Social Media Platform

# 📸 Connect, Share & Engage Through Visual Stories

**A modern social networking experience built for sharing and connection.**

Social Media Platform is a visual-based social networking application that allows users to connect with others through posts, stories, and interactive conversations. Inspired by modern social platforms, the app provides a personalized and engaging environment where users can share moments, communicate with friends, and manage their online presence.

## ✨ Features

- 📸 Upload and share posts
- 🌟 Create and share stories
- ❤️ Like, comment, and share content
- 💬 One-to-one and group chat support
- 🎨 Customizable themes for a personalized experience
- 🔒 Private and public account options
- 👥 Manage followers and following lists
- 🚫 Block, unblock, and mute users
- 🔔 Stay connected through notifications
- 📱 Clean, modern, and user-friendly interface

## 📱 About the App

Social Media Platform is designed to bring people together through visual content and meaningful interactions. Users can share their daily moments, discover content, communicate with others, and customize their experience according to their preferences.

The platform gives users complete control over their privacy and interactions by allowing them to choose between private and public profiles, manage connections, and control who they interact with.

The app supports:

- Personal content sharing
- Social engagement
- Private conversations
- Group communication
- Personalized user experiences

## 🚀 How It Works

1. Create an account and set up your profile.
2. Choose your account privacy settings.
3. Upload posts and stories.
4. Connect with other users through likes, comments, and follows.
5. Start private or group conversations.
6. Customize your experience with themes and privacy controls.

## 🎯 Why Use This Platform?

- Share moments with your community
- Connect through photos, stories, and conversations
- Control your privacy and online presence
- Enjoy personalized themes and interactions
- Communicate through seamless messaging
- Experience a safe and engaging social environment
